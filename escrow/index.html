StableLab · TrustLock Module

Payments held safely in trust

When two parties don't fully trust each other, TrustLock locks the money onchain instead of sending it directly to the other side, releasing it only when the agreed condition is met; with an arbiter in the loop, it offers a fair resolution path even in disputes.
Example scenario: You're about to pay a freelancer for a project. If you send the money directly, there's a risk they disappear before finishing; if they do the work first and wait for payment, the trust problem shifts to their side. With TrustLock, you lock the money and appoint a trusted third party (or yourself) as arbiter; once the work is done, payment is released, and if a dispute arises, the arbiter decides.

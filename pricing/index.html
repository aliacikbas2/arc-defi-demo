Pricing

Transparency matters

No hidden fees. Here's the real fee structure for each module; all of it is defined directly in the smart contracts' own code, unchangeable.
Core Tools: Completely Free
The platform takes no fee whatsoever on Staking, Lending, or Swap. Swap only has a 0.3% fee that goes to liquidity providers in the pool; this share goes to liquidity providers, not the platform.
💸 FlowPay
0.25%
Taken once from the total amount as a platform fee when a stream is created.
🔒 TrustLock
1%
Taken when a deal is released. No fee at all on refunds.
🛡️ VaultGuard
0.2%
Taken when enough confirmations are gathered and the transfer executes automatically.
🧾 VeloPay
0.5%
Taken when an invoice is repaid. The remaining 2.5% spread goes to liquidity providers.
Why do we charge fees? These small commissions exist to show that StableLab can operate like a real product; a concrete example of how a sustainable platform could work. There's no real revenue on testnet, but the mechanism itself is real and functional.

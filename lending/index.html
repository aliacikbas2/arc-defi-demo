StableLab · Lending Module

Get liquidity without selling your asset

Lending lets you borrow by posting collateral, without selling your tARC. You can borrow instantly up to 66% of your collateral, and repay any time to withdraw it back.
Example scenario: You have tARC but don't want to sell it (maybe you expect it to appreciate). You deposit it as collateral and borrow some amount against it; this way you keep your asset while getting the liquidity you need. When you repay your debt, your collateral comes back to you.

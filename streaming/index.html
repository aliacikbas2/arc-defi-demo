StableLab · FlowPay Module

Money flows over time

Payments don't always have to be one-time. FlowPay streams an amount second by second over a duration you set; the recipient can withdraw whatever they've earned so far, at any moment. You can also stop the stream any time and reclaim the portion that hasn't flowed yet.
Example: 1000 tARC flowing over 24 hours
0 tARC~380 tARC earned (after 9 hours)1000 tARC
Example scenario: You pay a remote team member their monthly salary. Instead of waiting until month's end, you say "stream 3000 tARC over 30 days". If the employee needs it on day 15, they can withdraw the ~1500 tARC they've earned so far; you've effectively paid right on time, automatically. If they leave, you cancel and reclaim the portion that hasn't flowed yet.

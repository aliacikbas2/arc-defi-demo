Instant swaps between two assets

Swap lets you exchange between tARC and tUSD instantly through a decentralized pool. The price is calculated automatically based on the pool's ratios (the x·y=k formula); a small fee (0.3%) also lets liquidity providers earn.
Example scenario: You have tARC but need tUSD (or vice versa). Without going through an intermediary, you swap instantly, directly from the pool; the price is calculated transparently based on the pool's current ratio, and the transaction completes in seconds.

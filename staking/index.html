StableLab · Staking Module

Lock your asset, earn rewards over time

Staking is the most common way to get paid for waiting in crypto. You lock your tARC, and a reward accrues at a fixed rate every second. You can withdraw your principal and reward together whenever you like.
Example scenario: You have tARC sitting idle. You lock it into Staking, leave it for a few days or weeks, then withdraw both the principal and the reward it accrued together. You earn based on how long you held it; a simple, transparent, time-based yield model.
